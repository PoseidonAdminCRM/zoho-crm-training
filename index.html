What’s New in CRM

This page keeps you updated on changes, new features, workflow improvements, and important CRM-related updates.

Updates Timeline

Mar 1, 2026 Process

Closing an Opportunity in Zoho

Opportunity Owners are now responsible for updating their own Opportunities when a PO is received.

  • When you receive a PO, update the Opportunity stage and enter the close details in Zoho CRM.
  • If you need help, a step-by-step guide will be available in the Learning Library.

Thanks for helping keep our pipeline accurate and up to date.

Feb 25, 2026 Process

CRM Transition Update
  • Salesforce is now read-only as of February 25, 2026.
  • You may continue to access Salesforce to view historical data.
  • All new records and updates must be entered in Zoho CRM effective immediately.
  • Zoho CRM is now our official system of record.

Thank you for your cooperation during this transition.
